CV

Descriptive combinatorics & measurable group theory

I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in Mathematics at UC Berkeley, advised by Andrew Marks and Robin Tucker-Drob. I study Borel and measurable graphs arising from group actions, and the algorithmic and structural phenomena they encode.

Interests: Borel combinatorics, graphings, LCL complexity on grids, amenability.
Support: NSF Graduate Research Fellowship; Simons Dissertation Fellowship.
