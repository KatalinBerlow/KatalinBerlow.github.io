I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in Mathematics at UC Berkeley, advised by Andrew Marks and Robin Tucker-Drob. My work studies Borel and measurable graphs arising from group actions, and the combinatorial, dynamical, and group-theoretic structure they encode.

Email: katalin [at] berkeley [dot] edu
Office: 1057 Evans Hall
Interests: descriptive combinatorics, graphings, LCLs on grids, amenability

Links: arXiv · Google Scholar · GitHub

Supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and a Simons Dissertation Fellowship.
