Descriptive combinatorics & measurable group theory

I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in Mathematics at UC Berkeley, advised by Andrew Marks and Robin Tucker-Drob. My work studies how combinatorial structure encodes dynamical and group-theoretic information, with a focus on descriptive combinatorics, measured group theory, and applications to ergodic theory and geometric group theory.
